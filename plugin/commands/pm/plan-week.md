Help me plan my week as a Product Manager.

## Context

Review any existing context in CLAUDE.md, sandbox/, and product-catalog/ to understand:
- Current team priorities and focus areas
- In-flight projects and their status
- Upcoming deadlines or milestones

## Process

1. **Assess current state** — What's in progress? What shipped last week? What's blocked?
2. **Identify priorities** — Based on team context, what are the top 3-5 things that need attention this week?
3. **Schedule key activities** — Map priorities to concrete actions (meetings to schedule, docs to write, decisions to make, people to talk to)
4. **Flag risks** — What could go sideways this week? What needs proactive attention?
5. **Set success criteria** — What does a good week look like?

## Output Format

```markdown
## Week of [Date] — Planning

### Last Week Recap
- [What shipped/completed]
- [What slipped and why]

### This Week's Priorities
1. **[Priority 1]** — [Why it matters] → [Concrete action]
2. **[Priority 2]** — [Why it matters] → [Concrete action]
3. **[Priority 3]** — [Why it matters] → [Concrete action]

### Key Meetings & Prep Needed
| Meeting | Goal | Prep |
|---------|------|------|
| [Meeting] | [Outcome] | [What to prepare] |

### Risks to Watch
- [Risk 1] — [Mitigation]

### Success Criteria
- [ ] [What "done" looks like this week]
```

Ask me about my current context, priorities, and what happened last week before generating the plan.
