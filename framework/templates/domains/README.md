# Domain-Specific CLAUDE.md Templates

Pre-configured workspace templates for specific PM roles. Each includes domain-relevant metrics, rules, and skill recommendations.

## Available Templates

| Template | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| [Growth PM](CLAUDE-growth-pm.md) | Acquisition, activation, retention, experimentation |
| [Platform PM](CLAUDE-platform-pm.md) | Internal platforms, APIs, developer experience |
| [B2B PM](CLAUDE-b2b-pm.md) | Enterprise products, sales-driven, account management |
| [Mobile PM](CLAUDE-mobile-pm.md) | Native apps, app store, cross-platform (iOS/Android) |

## How to Use

1. Copy the template that matches your role to your workspace as `CLAUDE.md`
2. Fill in the bracketed placeholders with your specific context
3. Delete sections that don't apply
4. Add company-specific links, tools, and constraints

These templates extend the [base CLAUDE.md template](../CLAUDE.md) with domain-specific structure. You can also combine elements from multiple templates if your role spans domains (e.g., Growth + Mobile).
